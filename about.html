品牌故事Our Story

本草香珠诞生于一个简单的信念：中医养生不应该只停留在药房里，它可以融入你的日常穿搭，成为一种生活方式。BenCao Beads was born from a simple belief: TCM wellness shouldn't stay in the pharmacy — it can become part of your daily style and lifestyle.

我们与资深中医师合作，将传承千年的九种体质理论融入天然药材香珠的配方之中。每一款香珠，都对应一种体质的调养需求——气虚补气、阳虚驱寒、气郁解郁……不是普通的饰品，而是戴在手腕上的中医智慧。We collaborate with experienced TCM practitioners to infuse the ancient theory of nine body constitutions into our natural herbal bead formulas. Each bracelet corresponds to a specific constitutional need — replenishing qi, warming yang, soothing stagnation… Not just jewelry, but TCM wisdom on your wrist.

每一颗香珠都经过精心选材、手工搓制。我们坚持使用道地药材，拒绝化学添加，让你佩戴安心、闻香养身。Every bead is carefully selected and hand-rolled by artisans. We insist on authentic medicinal herbs with zero chemical additives, so you can wear them with confidence and heal through fragrance.
